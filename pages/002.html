How this course is structured

Getting Productive (10 minutes).

We look at how to get productive, the time thieves that stop productivity and how to deal with them.

Pardon the interruption (10 minutes).

Identify your time thieves and use tools to help you deal with them.

The Put off (10 minutes).

Recognising that you procrastinate and how to deal with it.

Prioritisation (10 minutes).

Learn how to prioritise your to-do list.

Goal Setting (10 minutes).

Use Goal Setting techniques to achieve your goals. Learn about working backwards.
