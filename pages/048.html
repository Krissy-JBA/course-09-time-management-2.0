Do

It's urgent and important, so do it immediately and do it yourself. It could be a crisis, a deadline or a problem.

Delete

It's not urgent or important, so ditch it immediately. Even thinking about it is a waste of time!

Delegate

It's urgent but not important, so think about who else can do it. This might be an interruption or a meeting.

Decide

It's important but not urgent, so schedule a time to do it. This might be going to the gym, setting goals or spending time with mates.
